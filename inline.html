Reference

Robotics exhibitions showcase the latest advancements in automation and robotic technology, often featuring interactive exhibits and educational demonstrations. These events highlight the diverse applications of robots in various fields, from manufacturing and healthcare to entertainment and education. Some exhibitions focus on specific aspects like robot design, AI integration, or human-robot interaction, while others aim to provide a broad overview of the robotics landscape.
